Liberia Festival

December 17 to 22, 2025 • Monrovia, Liberia

LIBFEST2025 is a festival to celebrate Liberia’s upcoming 178th birthday as a nation. The aim of the festival is to not only celebrate the struggles and progress Liberia has made as a nation, but to also promote tourism among the Liberian diaspora and boost the local economy. The festival is targeted at local Liberians and Liberians in the diaspora. The 5-day festival will be held in Monrovia and is planned to run from December 17 to December 22, 2025. It will include a variety of events including sporting events, musical events, excursion trips to tourist destinations in Monrovia and around the country, and an investment workshop. Local businesses, artists, athletes, and exciting tourist destinations will be prominently featured throughout the festival.
