Meet the People Behind LIBFEST2025

LIBFEST2025 is made possible by a passionate and diverse team of organizers, creatives, volunteers, and community leaders—both in Liberia and across the diaspora.

Each member brings a unique perspective to ensure this festival is a true celebration of Liberia’s past, present, and future.

From logistics and event planning to media outreach and cultural programming, meet the dedicated teams working to make Liberia’s 178th Independence celebration unforgettable.
